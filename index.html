About

What do you want to know?

In 2010, Emily, a busy college student, had a big vision to help students and other individuals earn an income while sharing their knowledge and skills. Inspired by her personal experience as a student looking to make extra money, Emily started her innovative company in the education sector. Emily started the company with very limited capital, relying on her determination, knowledge and passion. He created an online platform that allows students, professionals, and individuals from various backgrounds to become teachers and teach a variety of subjects and skills.
      1. Global Reach: The company has successfully expanded its reach to more than 100 countries, connecting teachers and students from all over the world.
      2. Tens of Millions of Active Users: The platform has reached more than 30 million active users, including teachers and students, who actively use their services.
      3. Thousands of Teachers: There are thousands of teachers who have joined the platform, teaching a variety of subjects and skills, and earning from their content.
      4. Content Diversification: The company has successfully diversified their content, including learning videos, online courses, articles, and diverse educational resources.
      5. Tens of Millions of Lessons Learned: Over 50 million lessons have been learned through the platform, helping individuals develop new skills and knowledge.
      6. Strategic Partnerships: The company has formed strategic partnerships with universities, schools and other educational institutions to provide access to quality education.
      7. Technological Innovation: They continue to innovate in educational technology, including the development of mobile applications and more interactive learning tools.
      8. Awards and Recognition: The company has received numerous awards in the education and technology industries, reflecting its positive contributions to the education and career development of individuals.
      9. Strong Teacher Community: They have built a strong community of teachers, who support each other and share their experiences.
      10. Changing Lives: The company has helped thousands of individuals change their lives, either as teachers earning extra income or as students improving their skills and knowledge.
